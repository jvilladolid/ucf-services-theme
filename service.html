Daunted by dollar signs? Don’t be. The Office of Financial Assistance is your go-to place for understanding how to pay for college and make the best of your investment. Our team will help you search for scholarships, apply for financial aid, find student jobs and make smart decisions about loans. We can even help you budget for the fun stuff, like spring break and a semester abroad.

No matter who you are, you've got to reapply for aid each year. Need some help? Come by our offce! Financial aid counseling is available on a walk-in basis. To protect your privacy, we offer limited counseling services through phone and email.

There's more to financial aid than scholarships and loans! We also have positions available in athletics, recreation, classrooms, laboratories, the Library, offices, research, information services, and many other areas through our Federal Work Study program.

If you need financial aid and you qualify—75 percent of all UCF students do—the combination of financial aid plus scholarship funds can have a profound impact on your future success in and after college. Whether you're interested in learning about scholarships, loans, personal finance, or you just want to find out how to renew your financial support, we've got the information you need.

Additional Services

APPLYING FOR AID

All students must apply yearly for financial aid. To be given the greatest consideration, students should complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) prior to March 1st, the UCF priority date.

SCHOLARSHIPS

Scholarships are designed to reward, encourage and assist you in pursuing academic excellence and leadership roles. Many colleges and departments offfer scholarships to students that are specific to an area of specialization.

LOANS

UCF participates in the Federal Direct Loan Program. Direct Loans are low-interest educational loans administered through the U.S. Department of Education, which consists of the Federal Stafford, PLUS, and Grad PLUS Loans.

STUDENT EMPLOYMENT

The Federal Work Study provides part-time jobs for students on and off-campus. We can help you find a job that fits your schedule and is just minutes from your classes!
